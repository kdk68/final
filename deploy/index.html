Coupons good for $3 off the regular admission price are available for pickup at your local participating franchised new car and truck dealerships throughout Montgomery, Bucks, Lehigh, Berks, Philadelphia, Chester and  Delaware counties, as well as southern New Jersey and northern Delaware.
